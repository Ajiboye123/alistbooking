Pharrell Williams

Pharrell Lanscilo Williams is an American record producer, rapper, singer, songwriter, and entrepreneur. He is best known as one half of the record production duo The Neptunes.

  • Born: April 5, 1973 (age 51 years), Virginia Beach, Virginia, United States

Details:

  • Known For: Record production, singing, songwriting, rapping, fashion
  • Notable Achievements: Multiple Grammy Awards, Academy Award nominations, Billboard Music Awards, global chart-topping hits
  • Music Genres: Pop, hip hop, R&B, funk
  • Notable Albums: "In My Mind," "G I R L"
  • Other Ventures: Fashion brands (Billionaire Boys Club, Icecream), record label (i am OTHER), film scoring, collaborations with various artists
